From Visteon and University of Essex

Everyday Travel App

Everyday travel app is a project developed by the students at the University of Essex, in partnership with Visteon UK and Essex County Council. The application is meant to assist the users that have to travel between the same places every single day by allowing them to save their preferred routes and inform them in real time when there are incidents on such routes.
The app features consist of (but not limited to):

  • Login system
  • Favourite place search system
  • Favourite journey system
  • Real-time incident information

I first took responsibility in Android application maintenance and feature development. After a while I assisted in web server and iOS maintenance (for short period).

This application is only available in the UK
